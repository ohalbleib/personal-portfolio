About Me

Hello!

I'm Owen Halbleib, I have created this website to share my work and connect with others.

I am a 2023 graduate of Appalachian State University, where I earned a degree in Social Studies Education. I am currently employed as a Humanities Educator in Raleigh, NC, and I am a student in the MEITE Program at UNC Chapel Hill.

I am a Wake County native, so when I find myself some free time, you will often find me hiking the trails of Umstead State Park.
